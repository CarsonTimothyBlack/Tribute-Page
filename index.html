Pearl's story

Pearl was born on May 11, 2017, via c-section. She was a 1 on the first minute...hospital measurement stuff, which basically means she was in deep trouble that first minute. Very blue. Very not breathing. Very scary. However, she was a 5, the highest, by minute 5 of HMS (hospital measurement stuff), which means all was well. She passed all the regular tests:

  • She cried
  • She started turning from blue to pink
  • She allowed a suction tube to pull liquid from her lungs
  • She reacted to the "fall and catch", which is basically like a team building trust fall.
